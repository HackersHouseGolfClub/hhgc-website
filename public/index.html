The Club

A modern clubhouse for golfers who respect the game and still laugh at the occasional snap hook.

Hacker’s House blends premium indoor simulator golf with warm hospitality, local community, league play, private events, and a bar-centered social atmosphere. It is designed for players chasing better numbers, beginners learning without intimidation, friends looking for a night out, and members who want a place that feels like their own.

The experience is polished enough to impress and approachable enough to enjoy. Not a country club. Not an arcade. A real golf clubhouse built indoors.

Play & Practice

Premium simulator bays, reliable booking, data-driven practice, casual rounds, and year-round reps when the weather has other plans.

Compete & Connect

Leagues, tournaments, member events, referrals, and local programming built to turn first visits into regular routines.

Stay Awhile

Beer, wine, non-alcoholic drinks, sports viewing, clubhouse seating, and a hospitality-first team keeping the bar social, not sloppy.
