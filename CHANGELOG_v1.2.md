# Website v1.2 Change Log

## Release Name

**Pre-Launch Credibility & Detail Pass**

## Strategic Objective

Make the Hacker's House website as tangible and informative as the active competitor site while preserving stronger brand consistency and avoiding unsupported pricing, address, equipment, construction, or opening claims.

## Customer-Facing Changes

- Tagline-led hero and clearer western Lake Norman positioning.
- Three-bay launch model stated prominently.
- More detail on practice, social play, leagues, private events, corporate outings, and beverage hospitality.
- Interactive simulator preview.
- Interactive experience selector.
- Interactive membership path explorer.
- Expanded events and community programming.
- Detailed FAQ.
- Improved location and service-area presentation.
- Founding List preview without a nonfunctional form.

## Technical Changes

- Version updated to 1.2.0.
- Added scroll progress and sticky-header state.
- Added active navigation based on viewport section.
- Added accessible tab and accordion interactions.
- Added reduced-motion behavior.
- Added structured data and canonical metadata.
- Added privacy page and sitemap entry.
- Updated legacy redirect paths.

## Intentionally Withheld

- Bay pricing
- Membership pricing
- Exact address
- Opening date
- Simulator vendor
- Deposits or presales
- Final House Key terms
- Final operating hours
- Final menu or food program
